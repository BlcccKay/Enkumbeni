About

Description

Enkumbeni Higher Primary School is regarded as Ordinary School based on Department of Education (DoE) categorization system. But, Enkumbeni Higher Primary School is also regarded as Primary School based on Provincial Education Departments Performance Indicators (PED). Enkumbeni Higher Primary School is a registered in South Africa on Education Management Information System – EMIS (The EMIS Number is a unique, 8-digit number assigned to all schools by the Department of Education – DoE). The EMIS number for Enkumbeni Higher Primary School is 500137973. The school is located in Kwazulu Natal province, and is ran as Public institute. Specialization for Enkumbeni Higher Primary School is Primary Subjects. The institute is located in local municipality of Ndwedwe, while Ndwedwe is situated in Ilembe District Municipality.
